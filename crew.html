Meet My Crew!

I usually just fall asleep in the back seat or chew on my rawhide bone. One time we even drove for like 13 hours! I was really tired after that one.

We're the same age and we LOVE to wrestle all day, even if we're sleepy. Then there's Bailey. She's daddy's family's puppy. She's five years old like Copper. Mommy and daddy were a little worried that we wouldn't get along, but all I have to do is lay down and Bailey won't mind me. Sometimes she's even in the mood to play!
